What's next?

Jun 19th, 2013

  1. Refresh your forgotten GPG/PGP knowledge. Take steps to use it more often. Use OTR. Learn about harddisk encryption.
  2. Read about cryptography. Write a ciphersaber. If you didn’t understand the last sentence, don’t just read over it, assuming it’s nerdy nonsense. Use a search engine to find out what a ciphersaber is.
  3. Understand that no cryptographic technology is a silver bullet against the use of sheer brute force by a determined surveillance state. Cryptography won’t help you against information that comes from correlations and dependencies.
  4. Become politically active. Don’t become paranoid, use the greatest communication tool we ever had to free yourself.
  5. Build upon hope in a hopeless environment.
